# ADR NNNN: [Short title of the decision]

**Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by ADR-XXXX  
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Phase:** 0-6

## Context

What problem am I solving. Two sentences maximum. What forces are at play?

## Options considered

Number them. Short description each.

1. **Option A** — brief description, key tradeoff
2. **Option B** — brief description, key tradeoff
3. **Option C** — brief description, key tradeoff

## Decision

One sentence. The option I picked.

## Rationale

Why this one. Why not the others. What I gave up. What would change my mind.

Two or three short paragraphs maximum. If it takes more, I haven't actually decided yet.

## Consequences

What I now have to do, defer, or accept as risk. Be specific.
