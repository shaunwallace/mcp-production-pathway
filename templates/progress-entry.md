# Weekly progress log

One entry per working session. Append-only. Don't edit past entries, they're a record of how I actually thought at the time.

Template for each entry:

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — Phase N, Week M, session K (Xh)

**Did:** what I worked on concretely
**Saw:** what I observed, learned, or had surprised me
**Next:** the next concrete action when I come back
**Stuck on:** blockers, confusions, or "come back to this" flags
```

Principles:

- Write it at the end of the session, not the start of the next one
- "Did" is concrete verbs: built, wrote, renamed, deployed, debugged
- "Saw" is the learning — the single most valuable field for later review
- "Stuck on" is fine to fill with honest blockers; don't paper over them
- If a session was a dud, write a one-line entry admitting it rather than skipping
